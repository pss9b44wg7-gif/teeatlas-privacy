TeeAtlas Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

TeeAtlas is a golf trip planning app. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app uses and how that information is handled.

Information You Enter

TeeAtlas lets you create and manage golf trips, including trip names, dates, tee times, hotels, transport details, notes, traveler names, and imported itinerary content.

How Your Data Is Stored

TeeAtlas is a local-first app. Your trip data is primarily stored on your device so the app can manage your itineraries.

Sharing

TeeAtlas only shares trip data when you explicitly choose to export or share a PDF, packet, or TeeAtlas trip file.

Maps, Location, and Weather

TeeAtlas may use Apple services such as MapKit and WeatherKit to show golf courses, hotels, routes, and weather forecasts.

If you allow location access, TeeAtlas may use your device location while the app is in use to show your position on the map or support map-related features.

Imported Text and PDFs

If you import itinerary text or PDF content, TeeAtlas uses that content only to help build a trip draft for your review.

Data Sales and Advertising

TeeAtlas does not sell your personal information and does not use third-party advertising SDKs.

Your Choices

  • You choose what trip information to enter.
  • You choose whether to grant location permission.
  • You choose whether to import, export, or share trip data.
  • You can archive, restore, or permanently delete trips in the app.
